Data Science Challenges Include:
(i) Process Ultra-high-frequency data 

(ii) Select an appropriate modeling framework (e.g., modeling the realised volatility or working directly on the stock price) to develop a prediction tool

(iii) Use multiple performance measures to assess your prediction model

Industry Challenges Include:
(i) Understand the basic financial knowledge associated with volatility

(ii) Make the prediction tool interpretable for traders to determine a trading strategy

(iii) Use the predictor to understand how stocks behave and quantify differences between stocks in a simple way



(i) Learning basic financial knowledge to use the order book to compute statistics such as the BidAskSpread, weighted average price, and volatility;

(ii) Learning classic (and maybe advanced) time series models to capture the dynamics of historical stock price and realised volatility;

(iii) Modelling and evaluation of the data from Optiver for predicting the future volatility of all the stocks;

(iv) Making an effective communication product to describe how the prediction model works and its limitations.

Possible focus of the project includes

trying more advanced time series models ;
obtaining additional insights (e.g., cluster different stocks) based on the prediction results;
understanding the impact of various tuning parameters (e.g., weights) on the prediction results.
